Who we are

This is an independent information and review website about RetaFit and the wider “reta” / retatrutide topic. You can contact us at kontakt@tryretafit.com.

What we collect

We keep data collection to a minimum. We do not ask you to create an account or submit personal details to read this site. When you arrive from an advert, standard click identifiers (for example a gclid or fbclid) may be present in the page URL and stored in your browser's local storage so that, if you continue to the official store, the referral can be attributed correctly. This is not linked to your name or contact details.

Cookies & local storage

This site uses minimal browser storage for basic functionality (such as remembering that a pop-up was already shown, and the click identifier described above). We do not sell your data. If our advertising partners set their own cookies on the destination store, those are governed by that store's own privacy policy.

Third-party store

When you click an offer button you are redirected to a third-party official store, which operates under its own privacy policy and terms. Any details you enter there (such as name, address or payment) are processed by that store, not by us.

Your rights

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR you have rights to access, correct or erase personal data and to object to processing. As we hold no account or contact database, most requests will relate to browser storage you can clear yourself at any time. For any question, email kontakt@tryretafit.com.
