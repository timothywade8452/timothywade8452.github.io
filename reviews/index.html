Does it work? The honest answer

Let's not insult your intelligence: a capsule of green tea, guarana and niacin is not going to reproduce the 20 %+ weight loss you see in retatrutide headlines. Nothing over the counter will. Anyone promising that is selling you a story.

What a metabolism-support supplement can realistically do is provide a small daily nudge — an energy lift and a bit of routine — that helps some people stay consistent with the things that actually move the scale: how you eat, how you move, how you sleep. Think of RetaFit as a helper on top of the basics, not a replacement for them.

That's why our score is a 4.5 for a supplement in its class — not “4.5 = as good as a drug.” It earns the mark for being transparent, fairly priced, rebill-free, and honest about what it is.
